# Kaida – Story

A minimalistic, atmospheric story project focused on the character **Kaida**.
This website combines visual design, music, and narrative to create an immersive experience inspired by traditional Japanese aesthetics and cinematic storytelling.

---

## ✨ Concept

The project presents Kaida through multiple perspectives:

* **Prolog** – a vague introduction, leaving her identity unclear
* **The Sushi Master** – how she is perceived in everyday life
* **The Ninja** – her hidden past and true nature
* **The Child?** – the unknown origin she never speaks about

The visual design reflects this duality:
Kaida gradually becomes more visible as more of her story is revealed.
